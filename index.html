Verbatims — In Visitors' Own Words
All 287 open-ended “why would you recommend?” responses, translated into English. Green = positive, blue = neutral / mixed, red = negative.
What visitors value most94
  • The show's role as the industry's must-attend hub — 41 comments frame it as the one place the sector gathers.
  • Exhibitor quality and professionalism (24 mentions) and strong networking and new contacts (23).
  • International reach (17) and exposure to innovation, new products and trends (16).
Mixed & conditional views32
  • Value is recognised but qualified — useful as an industry gathering, yet several note relevance varies by sub-sector.
  • Comparisons to other editions recur: some see Orlando or IAAPA Europe as larger or richer.
  • Requests to keep up the breadth of exhibitors, education and forward-looking content.
What needs fixing28
  • Venue experience is the top complaint (8): heat / no air-con, long walks, hard car access and difficult navigation.
  • Scale concerns (11): the show felt small or had too few / too similar exhibitors, especially vs. past editions.
  • Operational gaps: ticket price, food options, weak education prep, and badge/WiFi friction.
